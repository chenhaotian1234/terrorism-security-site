About the Topic

Terrorism has evolved with technology, moving from traditional acts of violence to cyberterrorism and information warfare. States around the world have responded with varying degrees of surveillance, intelligence, and international cooperation. This site explores these intersections, raising awareness about how modern technology reshapes the global security landscape.
